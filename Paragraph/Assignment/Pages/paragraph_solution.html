Homer Simpson

A beloved character in The Simpsons

About me

I am the main protagonist and one of the five main characters of The Simpsons. I am the spouse of Marge Simpson and father of Bart, Lisa and Maggie Simpson. I am overweight (~240 pounds), lazy, and often ignorant to the world around me. Although I have many flaws, I have shown to have great caring, love, and even bravery to those I cares about and, sometimes, even others I don't.

My favorite color is blue and I enjoy dancing, eating donuts, hugging, driving a sit-down arcade racer, playing the piano and horse riding. My favorite kiddie rides are "Stuart Little", "Barney Train" and "Ride the Champion Mechanical Horse".
